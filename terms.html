1. What StatusReach UG Does

StatusReach UG is a marketing distribution platform connecting Businesses, who run advertising campaigns, with Sharers, who promote those campaigns on WhatsApp Status and social media in exchange for payment per qualifying conversation generated.

2. Account Types

  • Business: creates and funds advertising campaigns.
  • Sharer: shares assigned campaigns and earns a fixed amount per confirmed conversation.
  • Admin: reviews and approves campaigns, manages payouts and platform funds.

You agree to provide accurate information when registering and to keep your login credentials secure.

3. Payments and Wallet

  • Businesses top up their in-app wallet via Pesapal. The processing fee charged by Pesapal is borne by the Business.
  • Funds are only deducted from a Business wallet when a submitted campaign is approved by an Admin.
  • Sharers earn UGX 400 per confirmed conversation, up to the limit assigned to their campaign slot.
  • Sharer payouts are processed via mobile money or other supported methods and may be subject to a short holding period for fraud review.

4. Campaign Approval

All campaigns are subject to review and approval by StatusReach UG administrators. We reserve the right to reject any campaign that violates our content guidelines, Ugandan law, or appears fraudulent. If a campaign is rejected after funds were already deducted, the full amount is refunded to the Business wallet.

6. Fraud Prevention

To protect Businesses and the integrity of the platform, we monitor click patterns, IP addresses, and device identifiers. Conversations flagged as suspicious may not be paid out, and accounts engaging in repeated suspicious activity may be suspended.
