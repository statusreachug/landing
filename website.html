How it works

One ad, four people, one sale.

01

A business posts a campaign

A shop, salon, or service uploads one photo and a short pitch. No design team, no agency, no minimum budget.

02

A sharer adds it to their Status

Anyone — students, boda riders, hustlers — can pick it up and post it to their WhatsApp Status or social pages, with their own personal link attached.

03

A customer taps through

Someone watching that Status taps the link, sees the business, and messages them directly on WhatsApp — no app to download.

04

Everyone gets credit

The conversation is automatically traced back to the sharer who brought it in, so businesses see real leads and sharers see real earnings.
