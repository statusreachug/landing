Privacy Policy

StatusReach UG

Last updated: June 2026

StatusReach UG ("we", "our", "the platform") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: name, email address, phone number, profile photo, and location when you register as a Business or Sharer.
  • Campaign data: ad images/videos, descriptions, and budgets submitted by businesses.
  • Activity data: conversations generated, tracking link clicks, IP address, and device identifiers used for fraud prevention.
  • Payment data: transaction references and amounts processed through Pesapal. We do not store your card or mobile money PIN — this is handled directly by Pesapal.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and connect businesses with sharers
  • To calculate and pay out earnings to sharers
  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity (fake clicks, duplicate accounts)
  • To send you notifications about your campaigns, payouts, or account
  • To improve the platform and respond to support requests

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with:

  • Pesapal, our licensed payment processor, to process top-ups and payouts
  • Other users only as required for the service to function (e.g. a business receiving a lead's name, phone number, and message after they contact via the landing page)
  • Law enforcement if required by Ugandan law

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, an encrypted cloud database provider. We apply Row Level Security so users can only access data relevant to their own account.

5. Your Rights

  • You can edit your profile information at any time in the app
  • You can request deletion of your account from the Profile screen, or by emailing us
  • You can contact us to request a copy of the data we hold about you

6. Children's Privacy

StatusReach UG is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support.statusreachug@gmail.com.
